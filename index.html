Introduction


Tree Lover's Educational And Research Foundation (TLEARF) also well know as Tree Lover's Foundation is a registered NGO, located in Vadodara city of Gujarat State, India. The NGO is solely dedicated to environmental cause. Our crusadeis to reconstruct the ecosystem and conserve the environment by engaging the people in Social forestry, a forestrythat is by the people and for the people.

Mission


To enhance the green prosperity of our nation through social forestry.

The Tree After Care Team (TACT) which is an integral part of Tree Lover's Foundation, consisting of all well educated people with wide range of professional background have been working on every Sunday Morning from 7 am to 8.30 am since last 15 years for watering the trees planted by them and performing after care activities.

Miyawaki

Miyawaki is a technique pioneered by Japanese botanist Akira Miyawaki, that helps build dense, native forests. The approach is supposed to ensure that plant growth is 10 times faster and the resulting plantation is 30 times denser than usual.

Day Celebration

Tree lovers foundation people celebrate Birthday, Anniversary, World Environmental Day, Independence Day, Republic Day, Diwali, Dussehra and other day's celebration with tree planting, watering and cleaning activity.

Vruksh Prasaran

Free tree saplings of different varieties are distributed as "Prasad" from our tree distribution center. We insist 1.5ft deep pit to be ready before we distribute this tree-plant. We can distribute Rayan, Gunda, Ambali, Kadam, Billy, Neem, Kesudo(Palas), Jambu, Garmalo, Kachnar, Borsali, Umarado etc as per our availability.
