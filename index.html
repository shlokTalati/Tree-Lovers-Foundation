About Tree Lover's Educational And Research Foundation (TLEARF)

Tree Lover's Foundation

Tree Lover's Educational And Research Foundation (TLEARF) also well know as Tree Lover's Foundation is a registered NGO, located in Vadodara city of Gujarat State, India. The NGO is solely dedicated to environmental cause. Our crusadeis to reconstruct the ecosystem and conserve the environment by engaging the people in Social forestry, a forestrythat is by the people and for the people.

Benefits of Miyawaki (Shivawaki)

What is the Miyawaki Method?

The Miyawaki Method is a revolutionary technique for creating dense, native forests in a short span of time. Developed by Dr. Akira Miyawaki, a Japanese botanist and expert in plant ecology, this method promotes biodiversity and ecological restoration by planting native species close together.

  • Improves air and soil quality
  • Helps in carbon sequestration
  • Boosts groundwater recharge
  • Provides habitat for local wildlife
  • Reduces urban heat island effects
  • Creates green spaces in urban areas
