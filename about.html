About TLF

Tree Lover's Foundation

Tree Lover’s Foundation was founded by Late Shri Ishwar Krushikar nearly 40 years ago. The trust was then registered as an NGO on 1-07-1999 as Tree Lover’s Educational And Research Foundation (TLEARF) by the active help of Late ShriNanuBhai Amin, Chairman Jyoti Ltd. Since then the NGO is actively involved in environmental protection, social forestry and creating awareness about the same.

The NGO is currently headed under the leadership of Shri Anil M. Patel who is the secretary of the Tree Lover’s Foundation.

Vision And Mission

This vision reflects a long-term goal of building a better environment by combining awareness, scientific knowledge, and people’s participation. It emphasizes sustainability and ecological balance.

Education

Conduct workshops, awareness campaigns, school/college programs.

Research

Study best methods for plantation, biodiversity, and environmental health.

Engagement

Involve students, volunteers, corporates, and communities in plantation and conservation drives.

Action

Drive plantation projects, especially using methods like Miyawaki, and monitor their growth and impact..
